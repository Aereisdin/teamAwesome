Getting started...

We all know that in the beginning the hardest part is getting started. Doing the laundry, the dishes, raking the leaves. Web development is a bit different than the everyday. It really is hard to get started. Where are the resources? How am I suppose to understand what they are talking about? I am not even sure how to phrase this in a question...

This page was born out of the frustration of some humble bootcamp attentees. We wanted to make a launch point for those of you out there looking for a quick reference point to some great resources that will get you going in the right direction.

We Support Your Journey to Success

The links that are listed in the navigation bar will lead you off to resources for each of the individual languages we cover here. Hyper Text Markup Language, Cascading Style Sheets, and JavaScript. Within these pages you will find the collection of helpful information each of us has collected to try to make your journey an easier one. There are some cool Codepen demonstrations found throughout that will hopefully inspire you as well.
